Mission Statement

To foster a democratic and equitable living environment where all vulnerable individuals, especially underprivileged women and children, have access to education, healthcare, sustainable livelihood opportunities, and essential infrastructure services regardless of their economic and social status.

Vision Statement

To create a world where every individual, regardless of their socio-economic status, has access to resources and opportunities to build a better future for themselves and their families.

Our Focus Areas

Education

Providing scholarships, mentorship, and literacy programs to bridge the gap in education, empowering children and adults from low-income backgrounds to achieve lifelong learning and personal development.

Healthcare

Ensuring access to basic health services and awareness programs, emphasizing preventive care and promoting healthy living within underserved communities.

Empowerment

Creating sustainable livelihood opportunities through vocational training and micro-financing, enabling aspiring entrepreneurs in disadvantaged communities to achieve financial independence.

Community Development

Engaging in sustainable development projects that improve infrastructure, provide clean water access, and enhance sanitation facilities in communities.

Target Beneficiaries

Serving low-income families, children, women, and marginalized groups in both urban and rural areas. Rehabilitation efforts include providing food, shelter, and education for street children to help them become responsible citizens.

Founder's Story

Meet the visionary behind Multi Aid International Limited.

Edgar Mwanza is a passionate advocate for empowering vulnerable communities. With a deep-rooted commitment to fostering equitable living environments, he founded Multi Aid International Limited.

His journey began with a vision to address the critical issues faced by underprivileged individuals, particularly women and children. Inspired by the struggles of marginalized groups in Chitulika Village of Mpika, he developed a mission to ensure access to education, health services, and sustainable livelihoods for all.

"Together, we can empower lives and build a brighter future for the less privileged," says Edgar Mwanza, reflecting his unwavering dedication to community empowerment and sustainable development.

Edgar Mwanza's leadership and commitment inspire not only the Multi Aid International Limited team but also the communities they serve.

Our Services

How We Make a Difference

Multi Aid International Limited is dedicated to creating a positive impact in the lives of the communities we serve. We focus on empowering individuals through education, healthcare, economic opportunities, and sustainable development.

Feeding

We provide essential food and nutrition to vulnerable women and children, addressing food insecurity in our communities.

Educational Initiatives

We support education through after-school programs, homework help, skill-building workshops, and scholarships for higher learning.

Healthcare Outreach

We offer mobile health clinics, vaccinations, health education seminars, and mental health support and counseling services to promote well-being.

Economic Empowerment Network

We provide vocational training programs, micro-loan programs, and financial literacy workshops to promote financial independence.

Community Development Projects

We implement clean water initiatives, sanitation programs, and environmental awareness campaigns to create sustainable communities.
